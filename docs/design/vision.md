# Off The Rails — Vision

A co-op PvE expedition game where survivors restore a derelict train into a mobile home and battle platform, crossing the overgrown ruins of a rail-built colony world to scavenge, upgrade, and eventually escape the planet.

## Core Fantasy

You live on a train. It's not just transport — it's your workshop, infirmary, armory, and fortress. You push deeper into a dead colony's rail network, stopping to scavenge what you need, fighting what you disturb, and returning to your moving home before things get worse.

## Pacing Reference

**Barotrauma meets Project Zomboid on rails.**

- Mission-based expeditions with rising tension (Barotrauma)
- Persistent base you maintain and upgrade over time (Project Zomboid)
- Slow burn punctuated by crisis spikes
- Meaningful downtime between action
- Consequences that carry forward

## Pillars

1. **The Train Is Home** — Central progression object and emotional anchor
2. **Expeditions Have Stakes** — Leave the train, gather resources, extract before overwhelmed
3. **Roles Matter** — Professions create synergy, not hard gates
4. **Route Choice Drives Strategy** — The rail map is your campaign decision space

## Win Condition

Recover components, specialists, and schematics across the rail network. Construct or repair a spacecraft. Escape the planet.

## Scope Anchor

**MVP Target:**
- 2-8 player co-op
- One train (expandable via cars)
- 6-8 professions
- 5 location archetypes
- 1 enemy faction with variants
- Branching route map
- Campaign progression via key component recovery

## Scaling Considerations

- Core loop must work with 2 players (skeleton crew) up to 8 (full complement)
- Not all professions filled in small groups — design for overlap and coverage gaps
- Expedition team splitting viable at higher player counts
- Some train stations may go unmanned in small crews — automation or trade-offs
